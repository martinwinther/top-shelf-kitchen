steps:
  - "Preheat oven to 200°C. Line a baking sheet with parchment."
  - "Mix miso, 2 tbsp butter, and rice vinegar until smooth. Set aside."
  - "Toss broccoli with olive oil and salt. Spread on one side of the baking sheet."
  - "Place salmon skin-side down on the other side. Brush miso-butter mixture over salmon."
  - "Roast for 12-15 minutes until salmon is cooked through and broccoli is charred at the edges."
  - "Serve salmon and broccoli with remaining butter melted over top and lemon wedges on the side."

Don't overcook the salmon—it should still be slightly pink in the center. The miso butter will caramelize beautifully in the oven.
