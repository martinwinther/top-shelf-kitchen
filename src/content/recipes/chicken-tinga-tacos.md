steps:
  - "Heat oil in a large pan over medium-high. Season chicken with salt and brown on both sides, about 5 minutes total. Remove."
  - "Add onion to pan, cook until soft, 5 minutes. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds."
  - "Add tomatoes, chipotle, oregano, and salt. Bring to a simmer."
  - "Return chicken to pan. Cover and simmer 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through."
  - "Remove chicken, shred with forks. Return to pan and stir into sauce. Simmer 5 minutes."
  - "Warm tortillas. Serve tinga in tortillas with lime wedges and cotija if using."
