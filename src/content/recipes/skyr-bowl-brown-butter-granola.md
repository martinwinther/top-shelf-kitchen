steps:
  - "Preheat oven to 160°C. Melt butter in a small pan over medium heat. Cook until golden brown and nutty-smelling, about 5 minutes. Remove from heat."
  - "Mix oats, hazelnuts, honey, vanilla, and salt in a bowl. Pour in brown butter and stir until evenly coated."
  - "Spread granola on a baking sheet. Bake for 15-18 minutes, stirring halfway, until golden. Let cool completely."
  - "Segment oranges and grapefruit, removing all pith and membranes."
  - "Divide skyr among 4 bowls. Top with granola and citrus segments. Serve immediately."
